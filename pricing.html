QUESTIONS
Can I cancel at any time? ▼
Yes. All plans are month-to-month (or annual if you selected annual billing). Cancel from your account settings and you will keep access until the end of your billing period. No questions asked.
How is DFSLINEUPAI different from other DFS tools? ▼
Our NeuralStitch™ engine uses recency-weighted projections (not flat season averages), injury impact adjustments, and three lineup optimization modes — MEDIAN for cash games, CEILING for GPP tournaments, and MAXIMUM for high-variance situations. The projections update daily before each slate.
What sports are supported? ▼
NBA is fully supported at launch (April 1, 2026). NFL, MLB, and NHL are in development and will be available to Elite members first. Yahoo Fantasy and DraftKings are both supported for NBA.
How does the CSV export work? ▼
After building your lineup in the Pro or Elite plan, click Export CSV. The downloaded file is already formatted to DraftKings' or Yahoo's exact upload spec — position headers in row 1, player IDs in row 2. Upload directly to the platform with one click.
Is there a free trial for paid plans? ▼
The Free tier gives you full access to the player pool, rankings, and top pick previews — no credit card required. Paid tiers unlock immediately after checkout and there is no separate trial period.
When does my tier upgrade after I pay? ▼
Tier upgrades are processed automatically via Stripe webhook — typically within 30 seconds of successful payment. If your tier has not updated after 5 minutes, sign out and back in, or contact support.
