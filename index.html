Every other tool gives you projections. We give you the projection, the historical context, the injury adjustment, and the accuracy grade — then let you override it with your own read.

Usage percentage is the leading indicator. When a star goes down, the engine identifies who absorbs the minutes and projects the bump — automatically, before tip-off.
